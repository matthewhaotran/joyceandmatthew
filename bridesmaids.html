Bridesmaids

Nancy Nguyen

Matron of Honor

I met Nancy in high school but it was not until college when we grew closer and our friendship blossomed into the elaborate, enduring BFF bond we share today. From our lengthy brunch/coffee/Tea Station dates, to our novel-length texts, to our silly selfies to one another - my friendship with Nan is truly one-of-a-kind. Over the last decade, Nan has been by my side through events, big, small, and everything in between, so it goes without saying.. I am blessed and honored to have her as my matron of honor.

Favorite memory: One of my favorite (funny) memories with Nancy - circa 2011 or so, we were in Vegas with the girls & a group of older creepers came up and started talking to us. They asked for our names & everyone went down the line giving them fake names, once it got to me I was too flustered and unprepared to give a fake name so I said my name was Nancy, as I burst out laughing (busted my cover, oops!) then I see Nan next to me, giving me the stink eye after hearing me give out her name. We still have a good laugh about it until today.

Phung Nong

Matron of Honor

I first met Phung in middle school and over the years we have only grown closer. We were badminton doubles partners, study buddies, and everything in between. Phung is one of the silliest, dorkiest, smartest, most thoughtful people I know. I think we get along so well because we are very similar but she is just much weirder and outgoing than I am. I am so grateful to have her as one of my dearest friends for so much of my life, and now my maid of honor.

Favorite memory: I always loved cheering each other on as badminton partners, tapping rackets after a nice shot or a point, and being goofy on the court.
